College of Engineering Career Fair

When: Monday, March 4th and 5th, 2024, from 11:00 am to 3:00 pm
Where: UConn Storrs, Student Union - third floor

Looking for a job after graduation? An internship, Co-op, Grad school information? Then this once-per-calendar-year Engineering ONLY fair is the place for you. Bring plenty of resumes!

Need help preparing? Contact Theo Menounos, Assistant Director, Career Coaching & Counseling I Center for Career Development | College of Engineering at theodore.menounos@uconn.edu

Professional Attire required!
